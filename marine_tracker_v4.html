Non-Negotiable Rules
!
200g protein daily cannot slip. Everything else has flexibility. Protein doesn't.
OK
Grilled proteins always fine. Chicken, fish, steak. Rice and vegetables are solid carb sources.
NO
Fried food: once per week maximum. Sauces on the side — 200–400 hidden calories you don't see.
+
Creatine 5g daily post-workout. Whey protein only if you can't hit 200g from food. Nothing else.
Weight
Log your weight regularly. Morning before eating is most consistent.

Rules
Read these every Sunday night before you plan your week.
01
No zeros. Miss a session? 20 minutes of bodyweight at home that night. The habit matters more than the output.
02
Log every session. Workouts in Training, runs in the Run tab. If you didn't log it, it didn't happen.
03
Wake up by 9am every day. You train at 11am. Two hours to eat and prepare mentally is part of the program.
04
Tired from a shift does not mean skip. Tired means train. Injured means rest. Learn the difference.
05
Saturday long run — no headphones for weeks 1–4. Just you and the discomfort. That is where mental toughness is actually built.
06
3-mile goal: sub-27 minutes by week 12. That is a 9-minute mile. Your long runs will exceed 3 miles — the timed test will feel short.
07
200g protein daily on a cut. This is the one nutrition number that cannot slip under any circumstances.
08
Plan the week every Sunday night. Know your shift days. Schedule your sessions. No improvising mid-week.
09
Your friend is in Marine training right now. On the days you want to quit, remember exactly what he is doing at that moment.
Sunday Recovery Protocol
Hip Flexor and Quad Stretch
90 seconds each side. Kneel on one knee, push hips forward. Essential after heavy leg days and runs.
Hamstring and Calf Stretch
60 seconds each side. Hold steady — no bouncing. Protects against tightness from running.
Thoracic Spine Mobility
Cat-cow 10 reps slow, then thread the needle 5 per side. Opens up your back from bench and rows.
Shoulder and Chest Opener
Doorframe chest stretch 60 seconds. Cross-body shoulder stretch 45 seconds each side.
Glute and Piriformis
Figure-four stretch on your back, 90 seconds each side. Prevents lower back tightness from squats and deadlifts.
Box Breathing — 10 Rounds
4 seconds in. 4 seconds hold. 4 seconds out. 4 seconds hold. Marines use this for stress and recovery.
Cold Water Finish
End your shower with 60–90 seconds of cold water. Reduces inflammation, speeds recovery, builds mental toughness.
